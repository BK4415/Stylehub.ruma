Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to book?

You can browse haircuts and services as a guest, but you'll need to create a free account before your first booking is confirmed. This lets you track your appointments and favorites.

Can I choose a specific barber?

Yes — the booking form lets you pick between our barbers based on availability for your chosen date and time.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule from your account up to 2 hours before your appointment. See our Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy for full details.

Will my haircut look exactly like the photo?

Photos are a reference to help communicate the style you want. Your barber will consult with you to adapt it to your hair type and face shape. See our Hairstyle Disclaimer for details.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome based on availability, though booking online guarantees your slot.
